> When AI proposes and humans approve

## The HITL Spectrum
- Full automation (no human touch)
- Human approval (AI does work, human approves)
- Human review (AI assists, human decides)
- AI assist (human does work, AI helps)

## When to Require Human Approval
- Financial threshold rules
- Regulatory requirements
- Risk-based escalation
- Confidence-based routing

## Designing the Approval Interface
- Show agent reasoning, not just recommendation
- One-click approve/reject
- Batch processing for high-volume approvals
- Mobile-friendly for on-the-go approvals
